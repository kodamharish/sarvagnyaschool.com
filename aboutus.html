Who We Are?

The purpose of SARVAGNYA School is to educate empower,and enable all students to become caring, contributing citizens who can succeed in an ever-changing world.
SARVAGNYA School is commited to focus on high expectations, individual success,creating a community of respect and responsibility.
Everyone in the school is responsible for the education and growth of the students.
Growing in this context means the implementation to develop mentally appropriate activities and lessons All school members are responsible of the physical, social, physiological and mental development growth that occur in the students.
SARVAGNYA School is dedicated to the teaching and learning of High Academic standards.
